### Risks & Edge Cases

| Risk | Severity | Mitigation |
|------|----------|------------|
| **Multiple H1 tags** | Medium | Restructure to single semantic H1 per page |
| **Generic meta descriptions** | Medium | Implement custom metadata in Next.js `generateMetadata()` |
| **WordPress.com subscribe widget** | Low | Replace with custom email signup (e.g., Resend, ConvertKit) |
| **Jetpack form** | Medium | Replace with Next.js API route + email service (Resend) |
| **Nature image backgrounds** | Low | Ensure proper optimization with `next/image` |
| **Spreaker podcast embed** | Low | Use iframe with appropriate sizing |
| **Italian-only content** | Low | Consider i18n if future expansion needed |
| **No existing blog posts** | Note | Blog page currently functions as podcast landing |
